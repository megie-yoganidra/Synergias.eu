Delivery Stabilisation & Operational Structuring
International Digital Education Platform

Platform operating across multiple markets  ·  Operational restructuring during rapid growth  ·  Result: 20% improvement in delivery efficiency

A digital education platform was growing fast. Expanding across multiple markets while the operational foundations struggled to keep up. Coordination was straining under informal workflows and unclear responsibilities. Timelines were slipping, dependencies were invisible, and the existing processes couldn't support the scale.

Delivery was restructured from the ground up. Clear scoping, realistic planning, defined ownership, scalable workflows. Communication rhythms that actually held. Delivery systems rebuilt to support growth instead of constantly reacting to it.

Commercial Delivery & Operational Control
B2B Financial Services Environment

Mortgage portfolios averaging £500K+ per case  ·  Broker network of 150+ partners  ·  Result: 15% increase in departmental sales

A specialist B2B financial services environment. High-value mortgage portfolios, multiple stakeholders, and a heavily regulated setting where coordination gaps cost deals.

Delivery was structured across complex cases from intake through completion. Clear requirements, defined ownership, no loose ends. Coordination between internal teams, partners, and brokers was tightened, reducing friction and making delivery consistent rather than reactive. The broker network was expanded through structured relationship management and proper pipeline visibility.

Execution went from reactive to controlled. Workflows stabilised, the team aligned, and delivery efficiency improved by 20%, with releases consistently on time and within scope.
15% increase in departmental sales, stronger commercial accountability, and an operation that held together under pressure.
About

Behind Synergias
is Megie.

I've been managing complexity my whole life, long before it became my profession.

Travelling the world, navigating different cultures, dealing with situations that had no obvious solution taught me something no methodology ever could: that most problems, however tangled, have a way through. You just have to stay calm, read the room, and think clearly when others can't.

That's what I bring to every project.

I'm Megie. I'm a project and operations consultant with experience across financial services, digital platforms, and international teams. But the work I do isn't just the product of a career. It's the product of a life spent figuring things out in real time, across industries, borders, and situations where the answer wasn't written down anywhere.

I lead from inside the work. I sit with the team, ask the questions that aren't being asked, and stay until I understand what's actually going on, not just what the brief says. Then I build the structure that lets the work move. Workflows, ownership, communication, visibility. Not imposed from above, but built together with the people who have to live inside it. Because a team that understands the meaning of what they're building, not just the tasks but the why, is the only team that takes accountability seriously and will deliver measurable results. That belief is at the centre of everything I do.

I don't get stressed. I get curious. Every problem has a solution, and finding it is just a matter of staying clear-headed long enough to see it.

I also never stop learning. New technology, AI, evolving processes. I follow them closely, exchange ideas with other project managers, and bring what's genuinely useful into the work. Not every tool belongs in every project. But the ones that improve coordination, visibility, and control matter.

That is what Synergias is. Not a formula. A way of working.
